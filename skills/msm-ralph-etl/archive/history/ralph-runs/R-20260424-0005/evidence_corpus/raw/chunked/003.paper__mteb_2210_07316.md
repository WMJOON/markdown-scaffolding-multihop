#### 2 Related Work

##### 2.1 Benchmarks

Benchmarks, such as (Super)GLUE (Wang et al., 2018, 2019) or Big-BENCH (Srivastava et al., 2022), and evaluation frameworks (Gao et al., 2021a) play a key role in driving NLP progress. Yearly released SemEval datasets (Agirre et al., 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016) are commonly used as the go-to benchmark for text embeddings.
